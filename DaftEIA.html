“The Environmental Impact Notification (EIA) draft 2020”


The new draft EIA notification (2020) condemns everything that it is supposed to represent and dilutes existing environmental regulations that help protect and safeguard our environment.


The new draft EIA notification proposes to:
• legitimise violations by those who start projects without environment clearance,
• weaken the democratic public consultation process,
• reduce the compliance requirements on the part of project proponents/promoters ,
• increase the number of projects that do not require an EIA process at all,
• and much, much more
Thus opening up new escape routes for environmental violators & enabling "development" at the cost of the very source of our lives.

The coronavirus pandemic has shown us the disastrous consequences of encroaching upon nature and at the same time given us a window of opportunity to atone for our sins. But the draft EIA notification 2020 seeks to do its exact opposite & repeat the same mistakes that led to this global crisis in the first place.
Thus we need to raise our voices & say a resounding NO to this mockery of environmental law or else history will remember us as silent enablers of the horrifying 'crimes against nature' that will follow.
Let’s save ourselves some dignity and NOT partake in this conspiracy against mother EARTH.
